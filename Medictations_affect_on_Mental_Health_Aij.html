The Purpose of Medication

Medications can be used for many purposes especially for mental health. Medication can help reduce and potentially eliminate symptoms. They help prevent psychiatric disorders and can also help patients that suffer from substance abuse. While medication is not the only option, it is a big part of your mental wellness. Medication works different for everyone. It's good to be informed and to talk to your provider about the best option for you. Certain medication will work faster and some medication will require consistent use for results.

Side effects of antidepressants

Antidepressants are used to treat depression, anxiety, pain, and insomnia. The most popular form of antidepressants are selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors(SSRIs). Other antidepressants are serotonin and norepinephrine reuptake inhibitors (SNRIs) and bupropion. These three antidepressants are the most popular because they have less side effects. Side effects includes nausea and vomiting, weight gain, diarrhea, sleepiness, and sexual problems.

Different Medication and Solutions

Effective medication exist for most mental disorder. Ranging from antidepressants, antipsychotics, to mood stabilizers. Other ways to help our mental wellness is through psychotherapy. Psychotherapy is a way to talk out your issues. Therapy paired with medication can be very beneficial to your mental wellness.
